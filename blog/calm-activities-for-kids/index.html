Every child needs moments of calm during the day. Whether it's after school, before bedtime, or during a rainy afternoon, quiet time helps kids recharge, develop focus, and process their emotions. Here are 7 screen-free activities that really work!

Coloring is one of the best calm activities for kids of all ages. It develops fine motor skills, encourages creativity, and naturally slows down a busy mind. Research shows that coloring can reduce anxiety in children, just like it does for adults.
